2) Problem Statement

Most advocates rely on offline referrals.
But users today search legal help directly online and choose the first trusted result.

Problems faced by users:

Confused which advocate to choose

Fear about confidentiality & cost

Need quick response

Want proof of expertise

Problems faced by advocate:

No structured online lead capture

No consistent branding

Enquiries come randomly and get missed

3) Goals & Success Metrics
3.1 Goals

G1: Generate Leads
Users should contact the advocate in 30 seconds.

G2: Build Trust
Users should feel safe & confident to approach.

G3: Improve Conversion
Visitors → WhatsApp / Call / Form submission.

3.2 Metrics

WhatsApp clicks per day

Calls per day

Form submissions per week

Conversion rate: visitors → enquiries (target 3–8%)

Time to first enquiry response (target < 30 minutes)

4) Target Users & Personas
Persona A: Urgent user (panic mode)

Searching after receiving notice / police call

Needs urgent support, wants “Call now”

Persona B: Research user

Comparing 3–4 lawyers

Wants proof: specializations, office, experience

Persona C: Service user

Wants documentation like legal notice, agreement drafting

Needs price info and process clarity
